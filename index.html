How to Use GistIt!

Enter the the text tou want to summarize in the first or topmost pane.
To get the topicwise summary, you can scroll a bit down, near the second pane, to click the "Get Summary" button. The summary will be shown the second pane.
To get the pointwise summary, scroll a bit further and click the "Get Points" button. The points will be displayed in the third pane.
To get the most 'n' important sentences, type the number in the textfield near the last pane and click on "Get Importanat Sentences" button. The sentences will be displayed in the last pane.
Important Note: For each of above three functionalities, you can use the slider and click on the respective buttons again to increase or decrease the depth of the summary.
